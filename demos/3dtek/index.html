About Us

Premier Precision Machining

3D TEK CNC, INC. provides premier precision machining, serving a core spectrum of precision industries which includes Medical, Aerospace, MEMS Technology and Particle Accelerator Industries. Specializing in precision medical components and micro mold builds for implantable silicone components.

3D TEK CNC, INC is committed to customer satisfaction. We strive to be your preferred supplier. We deliver the highest quality components on schedule to keep your projects on time.

With over 35 years of machining experience, 3D TEK CNC, INC. is capable of machining all types of medical grade materials, including Titanium 6AL-4V, Niobium, Polysulfone and PEEK. Whether your needs are prototype or production volumes, tooling or assembled fixtures, 3D TEK CNC, INC. has the expertise to help you bring your products to market.
