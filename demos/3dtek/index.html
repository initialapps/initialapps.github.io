About Us

Premier Precision Machining

3D TEK CNC, INC. is a premier manufacturer for the medical device market. Specializing in precision medical components and micro mold builds for implantable silicone components.

With over 30 years of machining experience, 3D TEK CNC, INC. is capable of machining all types of medical grade materials, including Titanium 6AL-4V, Niobium, Polysulfone and PEEK. Whether your needs are prototype or production volumes, tooling or assembled fixtures, 3D TEK CNC has the expertise to help you bring your medical products to market.
