    Why choose a Board-Certified Dermatologist?
    You might find many "skin specialists," or self-proclaimed specialist but all our MDs are board-certified by the American Board of Dermatology. A Dermatologist is a physician with training and expertise in the diagnosis and medical/surgical management of diseases of the skin, hair and nails, and mucous membranes.
